Voice

Telnyx AI Assistants

Real outbound calls via Call Control. AI dials retention departments, negotiates live, and reports back. Not chatbot theater.

Research

Tavily

Real-time competitor pricing research. Scrapes current market rates to build leverage before every call.

Deep Research

Yutori

Autonomous browsing agent that researches provider sites, verifies account details, and finds retention department numbers.

Knowledge Graph

Neo4j

Every negotiation makes the next one smarter. Stores competitor rates, retention offers, and success patterns across providers.

AI Brain

OpenAI GPT-4o

Powers the negotiation strategy and live conversation. Polite, persistent, data-armed. Escalates to supervisors when needed.

Voice Safety

Modulate Velma

Real-time voice intelligence monitoring. Detects escalation, emotion shifts, and ensures call quality and compliance.

Deployment

Render

Production deployment. Express backend serves the React dashboard and handles Telnyx webhooks at the edge.

Bill Detection

OpenAI Vision

Upload a credit card statement PDF. GPT-4o Vision extracts recurring bills automatically. One-click add to Slash.

Runtime

Node.js + TypeScript

Fully typed Express backend with graceful degradation. Every sponsor integration works independently or together.
